PEPPER MAKES MINT BETTER

Installing XXX Strong Mint 2.21
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Remove all old XXX Strong Mint files from the Pepper directory (You should be installing this over a new install of Mint 1.2).

2. Copy new files to /mint/pepper.

3. Log into Mint, click Preferences, then Install (Under the Pepper heading).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Updating XXX Strong Mint 2.21 from XXX Strong Mint 2.2 ONLY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Overwrite /pepper/nathankunicki/xxxstrongmint/class.php with the new class.php.  No uninstall required.
