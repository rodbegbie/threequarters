Installation:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Unzip winterfresh.zip.

2. Upload the entire /winterfresh/ folder to your /mint/styles/ folder.

3. Visit Mint in a web browser <http://www.yourdomain.com/mint/> and in the
preferences window change the style to Winterfresh in the drop down.
